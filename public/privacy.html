Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

TrackMe is designed to be a personal ride-tracking application. When you use the application, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect your name and email address.
  • Location Data (GPS): To provide our core service (ride tracking), the app collects your location data, including latitude, longitude, and altitude, both while the app is in the foreground and the background during an active recording.
  • User-Generated Content: Any feedback or crash reports you optionally submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the TrackMe service. Your GPS data is used to render maps and calculate your speed and altitude metrics. Your account information is used to authenticate you across devices securely and synchronize your ride data via Firebase.

3. Account and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data (rides, GPS points, emergency configurations) at any time. This can be done directly within the TrackMe Android app or through our web management portal.

Upon deletion, all your associated data is permanently wiped from our Firestore database and cannot be recovered.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or location data to third parties. Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption and security protocols.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the feedback mechanism inside the app or the web portal.
